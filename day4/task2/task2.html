TRAVEL PLACES

New

SWEDEN

Kungsträdgården, meaning "The King’s Garden," is Stockholm’s oldest and most iconic park, located centrally in the city.

PARIS

The Eiffel Tower is a tall iron tower in Paris, built in 1889. It is a famous symbol of France and loved by many visitors.

DUBAI

The Burj Khalifa is the tallest building in the world, standing proudly in the heart of Dubai.

SAUDI ARABIA

Makkah in Saudi Arabia is the holiest city in Islam, where millions of Muslims gather each year for the Hajj pilgrimage.

NETHERLAND

The Netherlands is famous for its colorful tulip fields that bloom beautifully each spring.

EGYPT

Egypt is a land of ancient wonders, home to the majestic pyramids, the Sphinx, and the timeless Nile River.
